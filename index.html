Welcome To Athens

School Project

Welcome To Athens application informs new Athens's visitors who want to explore about the city. The app introduces people some famous places in Athens that you should check out. "Welcome To Athens" breaks down into four category: shopping stores, parks, coffee shop, and restaurent. Each category consists the most favorites and highest rates by Athens's residents, such as, Cali N Titos - delicious Latin American restaurent or Zombie Coffee and Donuts - tasty donuts with richy coffee.

FF14 Stormblood

School Project

I have been playing Final Fantasy XIV for years, so I decided to make an application that could help me to be a better player. "FF14 Stormblood" is an iOS application that helps players access quickly to the informations and the solutions of the new dungeons and trials which are the contents published in patch 4.0 of the game. The application also includes the tutorial videos that could guide players while playing.

UGATalk Messenger

School Project

UGATalk is a messenger application for the student to talk and communicate with other UGA students. The application features 1-to-1 conversation as well as group chat. All user credentials and realtime messages are managed by Google's Firebase.
